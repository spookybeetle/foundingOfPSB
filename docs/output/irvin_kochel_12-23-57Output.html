December 23, 1957

Letter from Irvin Kochel to Mary Behrend Irvin Kochel Letter December 23, 1957
December 23, 1957 Mrs. Mary B. Behrend
21 West Putnam Greenwich, Conn.

Dear Mrs. Behrend: Enclosed with this short note is a copy of the "Hanging of the Greens!" program which was a very successful program. We thought you might like to have a copy.

Under separate cover we are serding you a copy of last year's Year Book which you also may enjoy looking, through.

Thanke very much for your beautiful Christmas Card and give my regards to everyone.

Cordially yoursy Irvin H. Kochel Admintstrative Head THK: HA Enc,
