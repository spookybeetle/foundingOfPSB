Letter from Irvin Kochel to Mary Behrend

Irvin Kochel

Letter

December 23, 1957

December 23, 1957

Mrs. Mary B. Behrend 021 West Putnam
1Greenwich, Conn.

1Dear Mrs. Behrend: Enclosed with this short note is a copy of the "Hanging of the Greens!" program which was a very successful program. We thought you might like to have a copy.

2Under separate cover we are serding you a copy of last year's Year Book which you also may enjoy looking, through.

3Thanke very much for your beautiful Christmas Card and give my regards to everyone.

Cordially yoursy Irvin H. Kochel Admintstrative Head THK: HA Enc,
