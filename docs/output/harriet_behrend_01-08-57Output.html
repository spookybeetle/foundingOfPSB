Letter from Hariet Sayre to Irvin Kochel

Hariet Sayre

Letter

January 8, 1957
GLEN COVE 4-0145 GLEN HEAD LONG ISLAND, NEW YORK Jan. 8th, 1957.

Mr. Irvin H. Hochel Pennsvlvania State University, Behrend Center, Erie, Pa.

1Dear Mr. Kochel: I was very interested to see the orogram for the Christmas ceremony at Behrend Center. I am looking forward to being in Erie in June, at which time I hone to have my two sons with me. I do hope you will be there, as I would like to bring them to Behrend Center and have them meet you.

2With best wishes for the New Year, I am

Sincerely yours, Harriet Sayre Harriet Behrend Sayre.
