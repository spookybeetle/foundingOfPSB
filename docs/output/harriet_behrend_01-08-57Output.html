Letter from Hariet Sayre to Irvin Kochel,

Letter from Hariet Sayre to Irvin Kochel Hariet Sayre Letter January 8, 1957 This is a letter from Hariet Sayre to Irvin Kochel regarding a Christmas ceremony that took place at the Behrend Center in 1956 about a month prior. She then discusses a trip she will be taking up to Erie in June. GLEN COVE 4-0145 GLEN HEAD LONG ISLAND, NEW YORK Jan. 8th, 1957. Mr. Irvin H. Hochel Pennsvlvania State University, Behrend Center, Erie, Pa. Dear Mr. Kochel: I was very interested to see the orogram for the Christmas ceremony at Behrend Center. I am looking forward to being in Erie in June, at which time I hone to have my two sons with me. I do hope you will be there, as I would like to bring them to Behrend Center and have them meet you. With best wishes for the New Year, I am Sincerely yours, Harriet Sayre Harriet Behrend Sayre.
